    • Gate Way of India

    "memory is the treasure house of the mind wherein the monuments there of are kept and preserved"

    Renowned as one of the most iconic structures of India, Gateway of India, was constructed along the Mumbai Harbour in 1924. Located at the tip of Apollo Bunder, this massive structure represents a commemorate memorial.

    Facts

    • The archway of the monument is built of Basalt and about 21 lakhs was spent to build the arch alone.
    • The structure is an adaptation of the Arc de Triomphe in Paris.
    • The design of the monument took three years to pass and finally got sanctioned on March 31, 1911.
